Some of the leading street artists in the world are coming to Brighton Japan! The phenomenal Lady Aiko and Kozy & Dan will take up residency, care of Art Republic, to create pieces of public art around the city. They will also be hosting workshops at various locations. if you wish to take part in these, please join our mailing list here.
