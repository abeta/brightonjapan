For the opening day of The Costume Games we will be inviting you to come to the Old Steine Gardens to join with Lady Aiko and other renowned artists and create something incredible: a piece of installation art that will become the home of The Costume Games for the next five days.  

Lady Aiko will curate the evening with films, workshops and performances.

There will also be a very special MOSHIMO Supper Club featuring Japanese entertainment (with a limited capacity of 120 people).
